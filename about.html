•           VISION

    The Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Pinas (DFCAMCLP), as a public institution of higher learning, commits itself to educate and serve the less privileged but deserving students of Las Pinas City through quality tertiary education by emphasizing the importance of knowledge and skills honed through strong values fashioned from the best in human and filipino traditions. It shall strive to achieve responsible service that will contribute to the common efforts toward community building, national development and global solidarity.

  • MISSION

    To achieve their vision, we will strive to provide quality and relevant education, responsive to the changing needs to the city, the nation, and the global environment, integration instruction, innovation research, community partnership and creative production to produce industry leaders, productive and value-laden citizens.

  • HISTORY OF SCHOOL DFCAITI TO DFCAMCLP-SATELLITE CAMPUS

    DFCAITTI is an institution accredited by TESDA that offers Technical and Vocational education and training programs (TVET) under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). It is a quality training institute established in 2008 by virtue of City Ordinance No.817-08 . The Information Technology Institute is fully supported by Hon. Vergel "Nene" Aguilar , Vice Mayor Henry Medina and the City Council of Las Piñas. It is home to 2,000 students who graduated from Las Piñas public high schools,who are bonafide Las Piñeros. The School is dedicated to IT with 25 classrooms ably supplied with more than 400 computers units. Courses offered are : 1-year Diploma on Web Development , Graphic Design and Animation , PC Networking and Programming.
